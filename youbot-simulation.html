Overview

I completed this project robotics class during the fall quarter of my senior year (2022). The KUKA youBot has an end effector with 5 degrees of freedom and an omnidirectional base. In this project, I manipulated the youBot to grab a block and place it at a goal location. The configuration at each timestep was determined in MATLAB by:

  • Calculating the end-effector's trajectory based on the initial conditions of the robot and block, as well as the goal location.
  • Calculating the speed at which each joint must move (AKA the twist) to achieve the given trajectory with a simple proportional-integral gain controller.
  • Updating the configuration of each timestep with the twist calculated at each timestep.

The calculated configurations were simulated in CoppeliaSim.
