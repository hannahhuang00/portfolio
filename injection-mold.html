Overview

In junior year (2022), I worked in a team of 4 to design and manufacture an injection-molded assembly that would assemble with only snap and friction fits.

    What I did:
  • Designed and modeled a figurine and a mold for the different parts in NX.
  • Calculated tool paths using NX CAM.
  • CNC'd 2 pair of molds (4 molds total).
  • Injection molded 20 complete assemblies (after tuning injection molding time, pressure, and temperature).
  • Analyzed defects such as shrinkage, flash, and sink. Used this
  • Analyzed the 20 parts to determine the precision and accuracy of our process.
