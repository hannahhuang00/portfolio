Overview

I used the Formlabs Form2 SLA printer to make a custom phone case, which features a cardholder, side grips, and a stamp of my name. I used Tough 1500.

    What I did:
  • Designed the phone case in SolidWorks.
  • Sliced model for printing in PreForm (Formlabs' slicing software).
  • Printed part on Formlabs Form2.
  • Post-processed part, which included washing in alcohol, removing supports, curing, and sanding.
  • Iterated on the design 3 times.
  • Performed user testing to arrive at the final iteration.
